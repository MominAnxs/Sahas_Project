Our Key Technologies

Advanced Resin-Based 3D Printing for Demanding Applications.

With micron-level precision and smooth finishes, SLA is the go-to solution for industries that demand performance and repeatability.

Selective Laser Sintering for Complex Geometries.

SLS technology enables the creation of intricate parts without support structures, making it ideal for functional prototypes and end-use parts with complex internal geometries.

Direct Metal Laser Sintering for High-Performance Components.

DMLS produces fully dense metal parts with mechanical properties comparable to traditionally manufactured components, perfect for aerospace and medical applications.

Fused Deposition Modeling with Advanced Composite Materials.

Our FDM technology supports a wide range of thermoplastics and composite materials, offering excellent strength-to-weight ratios for industrial applications.

Binder Jetting for Sand and Ceramic Applications.

Binder jetting technology enables rapid production of sand molds and ceramic parts, offering exceptional surface finish and dimensional accuracy for casting applications.

Continuous Carbon Fiber Technology for Ultimate Strength.

Our CCF technology embeds continuous carbon fiber strands throughout the part, delivering unmatched strength and stiffness comparable to aluminum at a fraction of the weight.

Precision 3D Scanning for Reverse Engineering.

Our advanced 3D scanning solutions capture intricate details with sub-millimeter accuracy, enabling seamless reverse engineering and quality inspection workflows.

Multi-Material Polyjet Printing for Realistic Prototypes.

Polyjet technology enables simultaneous printing of multiple materials and colors, creating realistic prototypes with varying material properties in a single build.
